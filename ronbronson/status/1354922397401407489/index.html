The Post-9/11 Forever GI Bill is a bit of a misnomer. GI Bill benefits expire 15 years after someone leaves the service, the forever law removes that limitation, but only for people who served after 2013-present. For anyone (🖖🏿) who served prior to 2012, 15 yr clock still applies
